## What is VidiFlow?

VidiFlow is a free online video downloader that lets you save videos from all major platforms instantly — clean, fast, and without tracking your data.

No account. No watermark. No nonsense.

## Why VidiFlow?

- **No login required** — just paste a link and go
- **No watermarks** — clean downloads ready for professional use
- **Privacy first** — we don't track your downloads or store your media
- **CDN-powered** — fast downloads, less waiting
- **4K quality** — high-res where supported
- **Multi-platform** — works on iPhone, Android, PC, and Mac

## Getting Started

1. Go to [vidiflow.co](https://vidiflow.co)
2. Paste the video URL into the input box
3. Hit Download
4. Done ✅

No installation. No account. Works right in your browser.
