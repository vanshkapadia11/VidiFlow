## Common Issues and Fixes

**My LinkedIn video isn't downloading:**
Make sure the post contains a **native LinkedIn video** (uploaded directly to LinkedIn). If the post is embedding a video from another platform, download it from the original source.

**The link isn't working:**
Make sure you copied **Copy link to post** (not the profile link or company page link). The link should look like `linkedin.com/posts/...` or `linkedin.com/feed/update/...`
