## How to Download Twitter / X Videos in 3 Steps

### Step 1 — Copy the Tweet Link

Open Twitter or X and find the tweet with the video you want to save.

- **On mobile:** Tap the **Share** button (arrow icon) → tap **Copy Link**
- **On desktop:** Click the **Share** icon → click **Copy Link to Tweet**

The link will look like:

```

https://x.com/username/status/1234567890
```

## Why Can't I Download Some Tweets?

A few reasons a tweet might not download:

- **Protected account** — If the account has tweets set to private, the video cannot be accessed without following them
- **Deleted tweet** — If the tweet has been deleted, the video is no longer accessible
- **Incorrect link** — Make sure you copied the link to the specific tweet, not the profile or home page
